**Board of Directors 2026 Election Nomination Information**

Nominations are now open for Philly Bike Action’s Board of Directors for the 2026 term. To nominate yourself or another PBA member, use <a href="https://bikeaction.org/elections/board-election-2025/nominate/">this nomination form</a>. Nominations will close at the end of the day on **October 31**.

**The text of your nomination statements will appear on the ballot.** If nominating someone else, they will be sent an email invitation to accept or decline your nomination.

Voting will take place **November 14–21.** Voters can approve of as many candidates as they want. Top vote-getters will fill 10–15 possible seats. See the chart below for more info.

The Board of Directors is responsible for PBA’s financial stewardship, legal compliance, governance, and strategic planning. Board members serve in a fiduciary capacity and may be expected to publicly represent PBA. Board members are expected to be regularly active on our Discord server to successfully fulfill associated duties such as approving projects.
